About Roche

Roche is a pharmaceutical company based out of Basel Switzerland. They have offices in many countries across the globe, including their Mississauga office, which is where I worked for my co-op. Roche is the fourth largest pharmaceutical country in the world, and the leading provider of cancer treatments globally

Job Description

While at Roche, I worked as a Pharma Technical Analyst within their intern program, FutureWorks. The way FutureWorks operates is that anybody that works at Roche can submit projects to FutureWorks. A FutureWorks intern will then hold a clarification meeting with the project sponsor to learn more about the project, and then whichever intern(s) is interested in the project can choose to work on it. An example of a project that I worked on while at Roche was a sustainable materials database. At Roche, to manufacture the pharmaceutical medications, they use a bunch of different chemicals. And in an effort be more environmentally friendly, as well as cautious about the effects these chemicals have on people's health, Roche utilises a bunch of different spreadsheets and tables from different global organisations to make decisions on what chemicals they use. This however is very time consuming due to the number of sheets they have to analyse to make a decision. The goal of the project was to reduce the amount of time they needed to spend analysing these sheets by putting it all into one, easy to access database. My role while working on this project was to use python to pull the information from all of the different tables and sheets, and then put all of the data into a google cloud BigQuery database which was then able to be accessed by an app that the project sponsor had built.
